About Me

I'm Sally Ann Herward, a NYS AADP Certified Holistic Health Coach. I'm taking a sabbatical from teaching and coaching to pursue the launching of our delicious chewy granola treats, Healthy.Eat.Live.Pray! H.E.L.P. Bites. I was faced with amny challenges raising our two children with health issues including Autism, neurological conditions, Asthma and severe nut allergies*. I needed to find a tasty but healthy snack alternative our sons could enjoy for breakfast, after school or whenever they wanted a treat. As a woman of faith, I prayed and developed a nutritious recipe, H.E.L.P Bites. These bite-sized chewy granola treats are filled with great taste. Our children love to snack on them, but so do our friends, family and business associates. I decided it was time to share H.E.L.P. Bites with others seaching for a healthy, but delicsious snack alternative.
